What is ARize?

ARize is the world's first Augmented Reality Creation Platform, using augmented reality and computer vision technology to transform how you connect with the world.

    The key points of the platform are:

  1. AR Creator Tool: The platform has a built-in AR creation tool that allows everyone to create AR samples and publish it instantly with a wide variety of. The app is giving the ability to receive statistics, track and optimize AR posts to have a better performance.
  2. AR Scanner: The platform enables users to scan the target (image, 3D object) to have an interactive AR experience and provides marker-less AR.
  3. 3D Gallery: The platform has a built-in 3D gallery of AR content which users can use to make AR experience.

ARize AR creation tool allows everyone to create Augmented Reality samples right on mobile device without having any technical knowledge within 3 simple steps:

  1. Choosing the target.
  2. Choosing the content( video, Image gallery, 3D models)
  3. Publishing.
