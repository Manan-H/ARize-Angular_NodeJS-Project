About ARize September 1, 2017

ARizePlatform:

ARize is an Augmented Reality platform where everyone can create their own AR samples and use them both for fun and for professional purposes.

The creation process doesn't require any technical skill, it is as easy as making a post in any well-known social platform.

ARize is also a platform for 3D modelers and 2D animators to showcase their art in a new, innovative and easily accessible way. ARize mobile app is available for iOS and Android users.
